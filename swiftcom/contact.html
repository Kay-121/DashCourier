Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will I receive a response?

Email responses typically within 24 hours. Phone support immediate during business hours. Live chat average 2 minutes.

Do you offer on-site support?

Yes, we offer on-site support for business customers in major metropolitan areas. Additional fees may apply.

What information should I include in my support request?

Please include your account number, service address, detailed description of the issue, and best contact method.

How do I report a service outage?

Call our 24/7 emergency line at 1-800-SWIFTCOM or use our online portal for immediate outage reporting.
